This site is focused on art that isn’t really seen or recognized as art. I (Tasha N.) have collected graffiti from bathrooms and pictures of posters that are seen all over Auraria campus in Denver, Colorado. I think these things are art forms that are ignored. I believe that anything that has been done by a person should be considered art. It isn’t going to be everything people expect in art. Everyone has their own taste, and if it insults then shrug it off. Observe these works and give input in a forgotten or illegal (graffiti) that litters Auraria.


The Experiment Graffiti page was an experiment on Auraria campus. I put black papers, with the request not to throw them away until I could collect them in English and Spanish. February 18th I put blank paper in West Classroom bathrooms, the first floor and the second floor. I went into the Art building and put blank paper in a stall in the first and second floor bathrooms. Next I went into central restrooms and put papers in all three levels. Sadly, this experiment was only done in the women’s restrooms.
